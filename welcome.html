Welcome to NWTBible!

NWTBible is the reader for New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures. This translation is available at http://watchtower.org.

The New World Translation is available in 40 languages. Please select your language using main menu.

Bible chapters will be downloaded when you open them. Downloaded chapters will be cached and can be read offline later.

You can download whole Bible text at once using “Download Bible” button in the main menu. This may take 10‒15 minutes. You also have to download full Bible text if you want to use full-text search feature.

Now, please select Bible verse using leftmost button in toolbar and start reading the word of God!
